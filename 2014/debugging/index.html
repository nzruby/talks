Debugging - What we can do as developers when things (inevitably) break

by Megan Bowra-Dean on 26 Jun 2014 at Wellrailed

Most of the time we throw up our hands in the air when we see error messages in software, having very little ability to diagnose or fix them. However we as developers do have the power to do something. Megan talks about commonly used tools and techniques in Ruby on Rails that allow us to hunt out and squash the bugs in our code.
